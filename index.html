The "AI Pod" Delivery Model

Moving beyond Time & Materials. We engage via AI Podsoutcome-focused squads powered by a Token-Based Subscription. You pay for value delivered, not hours spent.

The AI Pod

An elastic, multi-disciplinary unit combining human expertise with autonomous agents.

  • Human Core: Dedicated AI Architect, Data Engineer, Domain SME, and Compliance Lead.
  • Agentic Augmentation: Pods are equipped with specialized Coding Agents, Testing Bots, and "Shadow" Evaluators.
  • Elastic Capacity: Scale pod size up or down monthly based on your backlog velocity.

Token-Based Subscription

A simple "Services-as-Software" subscription. Architecture to Deployment in one predictable monthly fee.

  • Outcome-Based: Tokens map to units of value (e.g., "1 Persona Deployed", "1 App Migrated"), not hours.
  • Rollover & Swap: Unused tokens roll over. Swap capability between Engineering, Strategy, and Ops Pods instantly.
  • Transparency: Real-time dashboard of token burn vs. value delivered.
